Tomato and Eggs

Tomato and Eggs is a common Chinese dish beloved by many. This meal can be made simply with just a few ingredients. Accompanied with a side of rice, the comforting dish is a symphony of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1 lb. of Tomatoes, diced
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic, sliced
  • 1 1/2 tsp. of Sugar
  • 1 Tbs of Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, a pinch each of salt and black pepper.
  2. In a large nonstick or cast-iron skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add whisked eggs and use a spatula to form large curds, cooking until eggs are mostly cooked, 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer eggs to a bowl.
  3. Return skillet to medium heat, adding a touch more oil if pan is dry. Add garlic and stir until lightly golden, about 1 minute. Add tomatoes, teaspoon salt, pepper, and sugar. Then increase heat to medium-high. Stirring occasionally, cook until tomatoes are soft and half-disintegrated and juices thicken slightly to a saucy consistency, about 5 minutes.
  4. Return eggs back into the pan, then remove from heat. Stir to thoroughly combine tomatoes and eggs.
  5. Serve over rice.
